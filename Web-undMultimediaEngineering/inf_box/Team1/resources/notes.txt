XML Schema Notes:

1)	The attribute "ref" from <element> references the name attribute of a
	previously defined (global) <element>
2)	The attribute "type" from <element> references the name attribute of a
	previously defined <simpleType> or <complexType>
3)	The types xsd:integer, xsd:positiveInteger, etc do not establish size
	restrictions, whereas xsd:int, xsd:long, etc do
